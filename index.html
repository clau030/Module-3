A Day in the Life of a
Super Chill Bunny

Daily Life

Lola

Lola is a one and a half year old rabbit whose
hobbies include napping, eating, sleeping, hopping,
and napping again.

Morning/Afternoon

After a waking up in the morning to eat hay and drink water, Lola takes a long snooze
under the coffee table. But that doesn't mean she's lazy. Multiple times a day Lola does
a cardio workout of running up and down the stairs to stay in shape.

Evening/Night

Tired from all that running, Lola takes her afternoon siesta, until it's time for dinner.
She eats her leafy greens in the evening and prepares for a night of digging up the
carpet and chewing on the wooden furniture. All in all, she's had a perfect day.


Diet

Lola's diet consists of mostly dry hay, as well as pellets and fresh greens. Her favourite
fresh vegetables are kale and basil.

Entertainment

Bunnies have a relatively short attention span and get bored easily. This means it's
important for them to have activities to do. Lola likes to dig on her dog bed and throw
objects with her mouth.
